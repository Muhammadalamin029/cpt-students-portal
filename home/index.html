School Details

school

Federal University of Technology Minna is a leading technology-driven institution located in Minna, Niger State, Nigeria. Established in 1983, the university is guided by the motto Technology for Empowerment and serves a large academic community of more than 25,000 undergraduates and 2,000 postgraduates.

At the institutional level, the university is led by Vice-Chancellor Prof. Faruk Adamu Kuta , whose leadership shapes the academic direction, research culture, and student development priorities across the wider campus.

Faculty Details

The School of Information and Communication Technology brings together the university's technology-focused disciplines and provides a strong academic base for Computer Science and IT education. Within the faculty, students build practical and theoretical skills across software engineering, cybersecurity, information technology, and related computing fields.

At the faculty level, the school is coordinated by the Dean of the School of Information and Communication Technology , who oversees academic planning, departmental growth, and the quality of teaching and student support within the faculty.
